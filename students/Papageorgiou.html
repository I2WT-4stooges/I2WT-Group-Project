My experiences there were very pleasant and I have made some of my greatest friends there that I still have contacts with most of them.     Army

    At age of 23 I returned from Bulgaria and it was time for me to complete my military service. I served in the Hellenic Army Aviation for 9 months and it was a great experience. I have met great people there and I still keep contact with them. An interesting fact is that during the first month of my service was the training phase, where every solder that was enlisted on the same period gets trained mainly around:

    • Army Discipline
    • Officer Ranks
    • Combat Tactics
    • Weapon Handling
    • Body Training and Physique
    • Emergency First Aid

    At first I was worried that i was going to struggle with that but I tried my best. Thankfully it payed off with a very good outcome, and I even managed to finish in the TOP 3 out of fellow 80 soldiers. For this achievement I was awarded, along with the other 2 soldiers, a special honour patch that was issued to me in a special ceremony by a Brigadier General. This boosted my self-esteemed and made me understand that I can achieve great things if I try hard enough!

    Present Day

    After I finished with my army obligations I decided to pursue my lifelong passion: Computer Science! I liked the idea of making an electronic machine do whatever you tell it to do (given that you tell it in a language that it undertands). So for now my goal is to finish college and making the best out of it, by trying my best and aiming for the best results!
